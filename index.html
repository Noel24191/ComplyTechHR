Frequently Asked Questions

We intend to give answers to the questions that are most commonly asked around our services

  • What is Statutory Compliance?

    In Inida, statutory compliance is laid down by the government of india for companies to follow. These laws differ at national and state levels.

  • Why Is the Statutory Compliance?

    To adhere to statutory compliance is significant for employers and employees because of various laws and regulations. if the compaines follow the then then they can avoid facing legal issues, penalties and fines. That's the reason companies need to pay attention to compliance.

  • How should an establishment or a factory be Statutorily Complied?

    Establishment or a factory be statutory be covered by means of applicable registrations and renewals thereon, documentations as in maintaining required registers, following return submission as is advised and by paying wages/salaries to employees apart from that of remitting financial statutory dues as stipulated by labour laws.

  • What is a Factory?

    A place where a finished product is created with one or multiple raw materials with or with out machine, with or without electricity and on having stipulated number of employees to perform its day to day activities.

  • What is Shop and Establishment?

    A place where a product is sold with the aid of stipulated number of men and if the product be physical the place is called a shop and if the product be a service then the place is called an establishment

  • What is Provident Fund (PF)?

    It is a Financial Social security element, where the employer acts as a facilitator and the government act as a custodian.

  • What is Employee State Insurance (ESI)?

    It is a Physical Social Security element, where the employer act as a facilitator and the government act as physical and mental care taker.
